FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



An electronic trainer, also known as an electronic development board set, is a set of boards designed for prototyping and testing electronic circuits. These boards are mounted on a circuit board on which many electronic components can be placed and soldered. These components can include resistors, capacitors, transistors, integrated circuits, sensors and other electronic components. Electronic development board sets allow for rapid prototyping of electronic circuits, especially those used in electronics, robotics and IoT (internet of things) projects. These boards are often used in combination with Arduino, Raspberry Pi or other microcontrollers, making these devices even easier to use. Electronic development board sets are frequently used by electronics and engineering students, teachers, hobbyist electronics enthusiasts and professional engineers.

Yap-Boz is a set containing different microprocessors and sensors. It is packaged in a plastic box with 17 different modules and two breadboards ready to be controlled via software.




It is prepared by planning to be controlled through the Yap-Boz software. However, those who wish can also program via the pins prepared on the modules.




You can use the Yap-Boz trainer to test your projects. With the plug-and-play mechanism, you will not experience any surprise surcharges.




You can do many projects such as measuring circuit with temperature sensor, printing name on LCD screen, RC toy car, ADC reading with STM, mini home automation with ESP. The only thing that limits you here is your imagination.




You can use our training set, which you can use in many areas, especially in R&D, to minimize the cost in accidents and problems that may occur. In addition, you will also limit the fees you will pay for different electronic components.
