Unlike electronic traditional training sets, Yap-Boz is a training set that is easier to use, efficient, time-saving and requires minimum knowledge. Due to these features, it enables projects at all levels from beginner level to industrial applications. Unlike the examples in the market, it minimizes the cost to be experienced in case of failure with its easily removable mechanism.

Yap-Boz is a trainer that can be used with multiple microcontrollers as opposed to the single microcontroller limitation of traditional electronic trainers. This, together with the plug-and-play mechanism, provides easy testing for projects.

To provide convenience in education for future generations by blending the simplicity and fun of toys with our knowledge of electronics and software.

YB-GDM

Yap-Boz Power Distribution Module is one of the internal structures of the Yap-Boz. This module organizes and manages the energy flow between other modules. It also balances the power distribution between the modules, ensuring efficient and long-term use of the modules.

YB-STM

Yap-Boz STM32 Module, STM32 "Blue Pill" is a popular development board featuring the STM32F103C8T6 microcontroller, part of STMicroelectronics' STM32 family of ARM Cortex-M-based microcontrollers.

YB-ANM

Yap-Boz Arduino Nano Module, Arduino Nano is a member of the popular Arduino platform and is a compact development board. It is used in many projects thanks to its small size and affordable price.
